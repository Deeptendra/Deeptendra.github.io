Estimation of Engel Curve for the population of Meghalaya

Onset Detection

Jan '23 - May '23

Instructor: Arnab Chakraborty

Developed an onset detection algorithm for audio recordings, utilizing short-time Fourier transform (STFT) and spectrogram analysis. The algorithm involved preprocessing audio signals, dividing them into overlapping frames, applying the Hann windowing function, and computing the STFT to produce a magnitude spectrogram. Spectral flux was calculated to identify changes in spectral content, followed by a thresholding approach to detect sound event onsets. An optional clustering step was incorporated to mitigate over-detection of onsets.
